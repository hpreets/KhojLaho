How to Play

  • The goal of Word Search is to find all the words hidden within the puzzle.
  • The word list displayed below the puzzle, will show you all of the words that are hidden on the puzzle.
  • To select a word, tap and drag through letters of the word in the puzzle. The letters must lie next to each other either vertically or horizontally. If youâ€™ve selected the letters of the word correctly, the word will highlight on the puzzle and will be strikened from word list.
  • The words are arranged vertically and horizontally only.
  • To complete each round you must find all the words in the puzzle.

Levels

  • The game has 2 type of levels - Practice and Challenge.
  • Practice levels is more of fun playing with no scoring or time limit. There are 3 levels for Practice where the maze size increases and the word too change from Punjabi to Gurbani.
  • Challenge level is where you challenge yourself. You need to complete the search in 300 seconds. Your top 5 scores are maintained for you to break your own scores. All words in this level are from Guru Granth Sahib Ji only.

Tips

  • If you cannot find a word on the puzzle, tap on the word in word list. This is available only in Practice levels and not in Challenge level.
  • On some phones, you might have to scroll to see the buttons at the bottom of the puzzle. Scroll using the white space below the puzzle.
  • If the puzzle becomes unresponsive or does not respond to touch or dragging on puzzle in a proper way, tap button to start that round again. In Challenge level, go back to Home and start afresh.
  • Tap button to move to the next round without completing the current round. This button is available only in Practice level.
  • Tap button to move to the game's main screen. This button is available only in Practice level.
