How to Play

  • The goal of Word Search is to find all the words hidden within the puzzle.
  • The word list displayed below the puzzle, will show you all of the words that are hidden on the puzzle.
  • To select a word, tap and drag through letters of the word in the puzzle. The letters must lie next to each other either vertically or horizontally. If youâ€™ve selected the letters of the word correctly, the word will highlight on the puzzle and will be strikened from word list.
  • The words are arranged vertically and horizontally only.
  • To complete each round you must find all the words in the puzzle.



Screenshot




Tips

  • If you cannot find a word on the puzzle, tap on the word in word list.
  • On some phones, you will have to scroll to see the buttons at the bottom of the puzzle. Scroll using the white space on the left side of puzzle. No scrolling required on tablets.
  • If the puzzle becomes unresponsive or does not respond to touch or dragging on puzzle in a proper way, tap button to start that round again.
  • Tap button to move to the next round without completing the current round.
  • Tap button to move to the game's main screen.



About Khoj Laho

The purpose of this game is to teach kids about Punjabi and Gurmat words in a fun and interesting way. This would enable them to start using these words in their day-to-day talks.
